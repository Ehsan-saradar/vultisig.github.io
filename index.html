PROBLEMS

WHAT IS WRONG WITH
WALLETS

Harware wallets are clunky

Small screens, small buttons, annoying interfaces. New users are frightened by setting up wallets, updating firmware or signing transactions.

Hardware wallets are honeypots

Traveling with hardware wallets is dangerous - they’re obvious what they are. Attacks on hardware wallets are focused. Some don’t even have secure enclaves.

Hardware wallets are dangerous

Lose one key, lose it all. Multi-sig is the solution, but does not work natively on hardware wallets. Users are easily phished, or forget their recovery phrases. Different networks (ETH, BTC, SOL) have different multi-sig solutions, making a single UX impossible.

OUR SOLUTION

How Voltix solves the problem.
Rich hardware

A modern computer or mobile has an elegant and easy to access user experience. It has secure enclaves, cameras, touchscreens and connectivity.

Familiar hardware

All users know how to use modern devices and they are not honeypots on first glance. They have full encryption and security.

Threshold Signatures

Threshold signatures are compatible natively to all crypto networks, and all DeFi. No expensive multi-sig setups. No single key is vulnerable. No seed phrases.

USER EXPERIENCE

SEAMLESS SECURITY

Vault Generation

2of2, 2of3, or 3of4 - choose your vault type. Key generation done on your devices. No device holds the vault private key.

Transaction Signing

One device builds transaction data (send, swap, DeFi function call). A second device confirms the transaction.

Re-sharing

Lost a device? Replace it easily without changing the vault.
